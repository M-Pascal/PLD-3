Climate change: refers to long-term shifts in temperatures and weather patterns.
Carbon footprint: this is the amount of carbon dioxide released into the atmosphere.
Global warming: Refer to the increase in temperature of the earth's atmosphere that is caused by the increase of particular gases, especially carbon dioxide.
Greenhouse gas: Greenhouse gases are gases in Earth's atmosphere that trap heat.
Afforestation: Is the establishment of a forest or stand of trees (forestation) in an area where there was no recent tree cover.
